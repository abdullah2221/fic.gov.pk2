Cath Lab

Catheterization Laboratory is the heart of our Cardiology Department. The Cath Lab is equipped with two Angiogrpahy Machines while the third is going to be installed very soon. At the core of this department is the latest, state of the art, SIEMENS ARTIS –Z digital imaging system. This is capable of performing imaging of heart, brain and arteries and veins of all parts of the body for adults as well as children. The system has total digital acquisition, storage and processing capability of all images. It has very advanced features like digital subtraction, super-imposition, enhancement and other processing functions which allow very high resolution images for diagnostic purposes.Read More
